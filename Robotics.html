Robotics

Robotics is an interdisciplinary branch of Engineering and Science that includes Mechanical Engineering, Electronic Engineering, Information Technology, Computer Science, and others. Robotics deals with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots, as well as computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing.
